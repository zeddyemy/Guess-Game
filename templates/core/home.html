How to Play

  1. Select a difficulty level (Easy: 1-99, Moderate: 1-999, Expert: 1-9999)
  2. A random number will be generated within the selected range
  3. Make your guess and receive feedback (Too High, Too Low, or Correct!)
  4. You have 10 attempts to guess the correct number
  5. Score points based on how quickly you guess and the difficulty level
  6. Compete with others on the leaderboard!
